FUJII KAZE

Fujii Kaze (藤井 風, born June 14, 1997) is a Japanese singer-songwriter and musician under Universal Music Japan. Raised in Satoshō, Okayama in Japan. He released his first studio album, Help Ever Hurt Never in 2020. His second album, Love All Serve All, released in 2022 topped both the Japanese Billboard charts and the Oricon Albums Chart.
